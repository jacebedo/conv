Welcome to conv!
______________________________________________________

To make a conversion, enter the following command: "./conv <start unit> <end unit> <amount/qty>"
Currently, you may choose from these units:

    Length               Weight
_______________________________________________________
nanometres  (nm)      milligrams    (mg)
micrometres (um)      grams         (g)
millimetres (mm)      kilograms     (kg)
centimetres (cm)      metric tonnes (t)
metres      (m)       short tonnes  (tsht)
kilometres  (km)      long tonnes   (tlng)
inches      (in)      pounds        (lb)
feet        (ft)      ounces        (oz)
yard        (yd)
mile        (mi)
